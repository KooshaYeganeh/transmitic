    • Welcome to Transmitic

    Transmitic allows you to securely share files and folders with people.
    Downloads can be paused or lose their connection, and the download will continue where it left off.
    You will connect to people directly. There is no 3rd party server.



    Getting Started

    1. Your Public ID

    Your Public ID is: X 
    Share your Public ID with people you want to connect with.

    2. Finding Your IP Address

    People need your IP address to directly connect to you.
    Search online "what is my IP".
    It will be numbers separated with dots.
    Share your IP address.

    3. Opening a Port

    This part might seem difficult, but it's not.
    A port is used with an IP address to direct internet connections to software (like Transmitic).
    In your browser, login to your router by going to either http://192.168.0.1 OR http://192.168.1.1
    Login to your router. If you have never logged in before, the credentials might be on a sticker on the router itself.
    Look for one of these sections NAT Forwarding / Port Forwarding / Virtual Servers.
    Add a new entry (Your router's terminology may differ slightly).

    Interal Port: X

    External Port: X

    Protocol: TCP

    Internal IP Address: Local IP address for your computer



    4. Adding People to Share With

    After clicking the "Continue" button below, you will be directed to the "Users" tab.
    Add users' Public IDs and IP addresses to start sharing with them.

    5. Sharing Your Files

    After adding users, click the "My Sharing" tab to add files you want to share.
